The goal for player is navigating through from entrance to exit in the maze if all questions are answered correctly.

Player will start at a random location in the maze. Maze is composed of different size of rooms.
Each room will have one or more doors. The player shall choose one door to precede the game. Once the door is selected, one question will pop up.

As a player, you must correctly answer the question to pass through the door and be able to access to next room.
If the answer to the question is wrong, door will be closed permenately. If no door can be selected, game will be terminated!
